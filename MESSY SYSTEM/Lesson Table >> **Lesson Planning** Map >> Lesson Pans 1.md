Lesson 1: Primary Shapes (Foundation) Circle: Eternal cycles, celestial paths. Deities: Shamash (solar cycle), Nanna (lunar cycle). Simple → complex: basic circle → multi-petal rosettes (6, 8, 12-fold).

Triangle: Cosmic mountain, reaching heaven, divine hierarchy. Deity: Enlil. Simple triangle → 3-level ziggurat → 7-level stepped ziggurat.

Square/Rectangle: Four directions, earth, order. Deity: Anu. Simple: basic square/rectangle → complex urban grid plan.

Crescent Forms Crescent (Lunar): Moon phases, wisdom, cycles. Deity: Nanna/Sin. Simple arc → complex crescent with stars/horns.

Star Forms (Multi-Pointed) 8-Pointed Star: Venus cycle, Ishtar’s powers. Deity: Inanna/Ishtar. Simple: connect-the-dots → complex rosette/petals.

6-Pointed Star: Hexagonal symmetry, nature. Deity: various/nature. Basic star → hexagonal rosette.

12-Pointed Star: Year cycle, zodiac. Deities: zodiac gods. Simple: 12 equal rays → zodiac wheel.

Wave & Pattern Forms Zigzag: Water, rivers, life force, wisdom. Deity: Enki. Simple zigzag → intertwined guilloche pattern.

Meander/Spiral: Journey, transformation. Deity: Ningishzida. Simple spiral → double/intertwined serpents.

3D Forms Cylinder: Eternal rolling, administrative order. Deities: various (seals). Basic cylinder → carved narrative seal.

Cone: Focus point, divine light. Deity: solar deities. Simple cone → conical spire/bands.

Composite Symbols Combined Geometric Forms: Accumulated powers, multiple attributes. Major gods. Two shapes combined → multi-symbol layered deity badge.
