## 🎯 Project Overview

**Mission**: Build an integrated K-8 curriculum that uses geometric thinking as a bridge between pure art and pure mathematics to contextualize and deepen historical learning across fourteen civilizational periods.

**Core Innovation**: Using semiotics, design thinking, and mathematical/logical spatial reasoning to understand the causes of and thinking behind inventions across human history.
