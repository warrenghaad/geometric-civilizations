A visual journey through geometric pattern evolution across civilizations and time

About This Visualization

  • Structure: Concentric rings represent historical eras from prehistory (center) to modern computational age (outer)
  • Sectors: Different radial sections represent various civilizations and their contributions
  • Icons: Geometric motifs characteristic of each civilization and era
  • Arrow: Indicates the progression from observational patterns to formal and generative systems
  • Layers: The visualization uses proper SVG layering to ensure all elements are visible:
    • Layer 1: Background gradient
    • Layer 2: Title and subtitle text
    • Layer 3: Concentric ring circles
    • Layer 4: Radial arrow (semi-transparent)
    • Layer 5: Cultural and historical icons
    • Layer 6: Text labels (always on top)
