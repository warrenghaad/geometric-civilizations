## A4: Geometric Analysis - Right Triangle Properties
**Duration:** 8-10 minutes  
**Learning Objective:** Students will discover and measure the special properties that make right triangles essential for stable construction and accurate measurement.

### Exploring Right Triangle Geometry
Using rulers, protractors, and construction materials, students investigate:

**Right Triangle Elements:**
- **Right Angle:** The perfect 90-degree corner that defines the right triangle
- **Hypotenuse:** The longest side opposite the right angle
- **Legs:** The two sides that meet at the right angle
- **Proportional Relationships:** How the three sides relate to each other mathematically

### Hands-On Investigation
**Right Triangle Testing:**
1. **Angle Verification:** Measure angles in right triangles - confirm 90-degree corners
2. **Stability Testing:** Compare structures built with right triangles vs. other triangles
3. **Construction Applications:** Use right triangles to create square corners and stable foundations
4. **Measurement Tool Creation:** Make square-checking tools using right triangle principles

**Mathematical Discoveries:**
- Right triangles always have exactly one 90-degree angle
- The other two angles in a right triangle always add up to 90 degrees
- Right triangles can be used to create perfect square corners
- Right triangle relationships help solve measurement problems

**Decomposition Activity:** Break down right triangles into functional components:
- Right angle corner (provides stability and perfect square reference)
- Two legs (create the foundation base and height)
- Hypotenuse (connects the legs and provides structural integrity)
- Proportional relationships (mathematical properties that enable calculations)

**Artifacts Required:**
- Protractors, rulers, and square-checking tools for precise measurement
