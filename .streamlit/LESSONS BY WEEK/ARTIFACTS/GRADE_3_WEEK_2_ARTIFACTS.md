# Grade 3 Week 2: SIN & THE CRESCENT - Artifact Requirements

## STEM History Components (6 items)

1. **Sin's Crescent Moon Symbols and Ancient Lunar Calendars**
   - Moon phase charts showing crescent growth patterns
   - Images of ancient Sin moon god representations
   - Calendar examples showing lunar month divisions

2. **Timeline Materials Showing Curved Tool Development**
   - Pictures of ancient sickles, curved boats, arched buildings
   - Evolution from straight to curved cutting tool innovations
   - Chronological examples of curved architectural developments

3. **Cross-Cultural Examples of Curved Architectural Innovations**
   - Maps showing spread of arch and dome technologies
   - Examples of curved designs from Egypt, Rome, Asia
   - Comparison of cultural variations in curved building techniques

## Invention-Related Materials (7 items)

4. **Crescent Construction and Arc Measurement Tools**
   - Paper circles and crescents for cutting and folding
   - String and measuring tools for curve measurement
   - Simple protractors or angle guides for arc exploration

5. **Materials for Testing Curved vs. Straight Structural Strength**
   - Paper and tape for making bridges and arches
   - Toy cars for weight testing bridge structures
   - Play dough for building and testing cave structures

6. **Flow Testing Supplies for Curved Channel Experiments**
   - Cardboard and tape for building water channels
   - Water and containers for flow testing
   - Marbles and balls for movement experiments

7. **Examples of Curved vs. Angular Technology Comparisons**
   - Various curved and angular tools for comparison
   - Examples of curved handles, edges, and interfaces
   - Safety examples showing curved vs. sharp edge benefits

## General Project Objects (13 items)

8. **Classroom Manipulatives for Crescent Exploration**
   - Various sized bowls and curved containers
   - String, rulers, and measuring tools for curve analysis
   - Chart paper for recording curve pattern discoveries

9. **Art and Construction Supplies for Curved Design Challenges**
   - Drawing paper and art supplies for curve-based designs
   - Cardboard, tape, and containers for curved system building
   - Materials for creating curved architectural models

10. **Water and Flow Testing Materials**
    - Safe water and pouring containers for flow experiments
    - Materials for building curved water channels
    - Testing supplies for comparing flow effectiveness

## Total Artifacts Required: 26 items

## Key Learning Applications
These artifacts enable Grade 3 students to explore how curved shapes provide gentleness, flow efficiency, and natural adaptation - supporting both mathematical understanding and social-emotional learning about patience and gradual growth.
