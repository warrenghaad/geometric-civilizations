## Setup and Storage Recommendations

### Pre-Class Preparation
- Organize modular building components in accessible containers
- Prepare templates in multiple sizes for different scaling exercises
- Set up areas for both individual and large-group modular construction
- Test all calculation tools and templates for accuracy

### Safety Considerations
- Supervise use of cutting tools for template creation
- Ensure adequate space for large modular construction activities
- Check building components for appropriate size and safety
- Maintain organized storage to prevent loss of small components

### Storage Solutions
- Large bins for modular building components with clear labeling
- Flat storage for templates and design materials
- Rolling carts for heavy materials and supplies
- Digital archive for design templates and calculation tools
