## Setup and Storage Recommendations

### Pre-Class Preparation
- Pre-measure string lengths for consistent circle construction
- Test all measuring tools for accuracy and age-appropriate use
- Organize materials in labeled containers for easy distribution
- Prepare digital presentations with circular design examples

### Safety Considerations
- Supervise compass point usage during construction activities
- Ensure clean, safe objects for hands-on handling and measurement
- Maintain clear walkways during floor circle activities
- Check that all containers and manipulatives have smooth edges

### Storage Solutions
- Rolling cart for heavier materials and measuring tools
- Labeled bins for small objects and manipulatives
- Digital folder for image collections and visual examples
- Secure storage for precision measurement tools
