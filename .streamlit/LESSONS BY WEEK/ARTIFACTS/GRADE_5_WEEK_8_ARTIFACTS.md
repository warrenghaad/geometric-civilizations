## Setup and Storage Recommendations

### Pre-Class Preparation
- Calibrate and organize all precision measurement instruments
- Prepare materials for systematic accuracy testing and comparison
- Set up precision measurement stations with appropriate lighting
- Test all measurement tools for accuracy and proper function

### Safety Considerations
- Handle precision instruments carefully to maintain calibration
- Ensure proper lighting for accurate measurement activities
- Organize workspace to prevent damage to precision tools
- Maintain systematic organization to prevent loss of small precision components

### Storage Solutions
- Climate-controlled storage for precision instruments
- Organized storage system with individual compartments for measurement tools
- Secure storage for valuable precision equipment
- Digital archive for precision examples and technical documentation
