## Setup and Storage Recommendations

### Pre-Class Preparation
- Organize testing materials in waterproof containers
- Prepare geometric construction templates and guides
- Set up areas for both wet and dry activities
- Test all measurement tools for accuracy and consistency

### Safety Considerations
- Waterproof surfaces for clay and water activities
- Inspect all tools and materials for safe handling
- Ensure adequate ventilation for any dusty materials
- Maintain clear organization to prevent accidents with small parts

### Storage Solutions
- Waterproof containers for wet/messy materials
- Flat storage for maps, diagrams, and technical drawings
- Tool organization system for measurement instruments
- Digital archive for images and technical resources
