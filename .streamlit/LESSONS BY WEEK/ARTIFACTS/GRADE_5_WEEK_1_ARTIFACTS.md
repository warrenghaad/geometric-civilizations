## Setup and Storage Recommendations

### Pre-Class Preparation
- Pre-cut cardboard shapes to standard sizes
- Test all measuring tools for accuracy
- Organize materials in labeled containers
- Prepare digital presentations with images

### Safety Considerations
- Supervise compass point usage
- Ensure clean, safe objects for handling
- Check weights are appropriate for student age
- Maintain clear walkways during floor activities

### Storage Solutions
- Rolling cart for heavy materials
- Labeled bins for small objects
- Digital folder for image collections
- Secure storage for measurement tools
