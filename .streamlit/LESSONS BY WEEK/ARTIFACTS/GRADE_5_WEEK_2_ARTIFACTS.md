## Setup and Storage Recommendations

### Pre-Class Preparation
- Test water flow experiments for optimal flow rates
- Pre-cut crescent shapes to standard sizes for consistency
- Set up protected areas for water-based activities
- Organize cyclical demonstration materials

### Safety Considerations
- Waterproof surfaces and cleanup materials ready
- Inspect all tools for safe handling conditions
- Ensure adequate ventilation for any wet activities
- Clear walkways for dynamic testing activities

### Storage Solutions
- Waterproof containers for flow testing materials
- Organized tool collection with safety checks
- Digital archive for astronomical images and videos
- Labeled bins for cyclical demonstration materials
