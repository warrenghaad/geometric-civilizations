  <section id="thesis-voices">
    <h2>Stakeholder Thesis Voices</h2>
    <div class="grid g3">
      <div class="role"><strong>Student (K–2):</strong> “I can see shapes in stories and make my own pattern tales.”</div>
      <div class="role"><strong>Student (3–5):</strong> “I remix old myths with math patterns to explain ideas clearly.”</div>
      <div class="role"><strong>Student (6–8):</strong> “I prove why patterns work, adapt myths to new problems, and explain my design choices.”</div>
      <div class="role"><strong>Teacher:</strong> “I facilitate cross-domain pattern noticing that deepens literacy and reasoning simultaneously.”</div>
      <div class="role"><strong>Administrator:</strong> “We see measurable gains in ELA comprehension and math reasoning because students practice transfer daily.”</div>
      <div class="role"><strong>Family:</strong> “My child connects cultural stories to science and math and explains their thinking respectfully.”</div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section id="vertical-threads">
    <h2>Vertical Threads (K–8 Longitudinal Concepts)</h2>
    <div class="grid g3">
      <div class="vertical-thread"><strong>Pattern Recognition → Proof:</strong> K–2 identify & describe; 3–5 classify & transform; 6–8 generalize & justify.</div>
      <div class="vertical-thread"><strong>Myth Structure → Analytical Writing:</strong> Oral retell → compare motif → structural analysis → thematic synthesis.</div>
      <div class="vertical-thread"><strong>Symbol → Model:</strong> Draw → label → encode → abstract into variable/formula representation.</div>
      <div class="vertical-thread"><strong>Global Motifs & Equity:</strong> Hear → respect → connect → critique representation & bias.</div>
      <div class="vertical-thread"><strong>Creative Remix → Innovation:</strong> Combine shapes + myth → adapt culturally responsibly → prototype explanatory artifact.</div>
      <div class="vertical-thread"><strong>Mindful Meta-Cognition:</strong> Name feeling → regulate focus → select strategy → reflect & refine.</div>
    </div>
  </section>
